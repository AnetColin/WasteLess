### Project Description
WasteLess Smart Kitchen is a dynamic web app that helps users track food items in their kitchen by adding name, quantity, and expiry dates. It displays real‚Äëtime status (Fresh / At Risk / Expired), generates simple prediction stats, and highlights items nearing expiry in a colorful, animated UI. This tool helps reduce food waste and keeps users informed about their pantry at a glance. 

### The Problem statement
Despite growing awareness of sustainability, household food waste remains a major problem. Globally, households are responsible for a large share of food waste ‚Äî with more than one billion meals discarded every day, contributing to about 8‚Äì10% of global greenhouse gas emissions and wasting valuable resources like land, water, and energy.
At the consumer level, people often forget what food they have, misinterpret expiry dates, and lack simple tools to track and manage their pantry items. As a result, edible food is thrown away unnecessarily, increasing both everyday waste and environmental impact.
WasteLess Smart Kitchen addresses this challenge by giving users a dynamic way to track the food they already have, monitor expiry dates, and reduce preventable waste through organized pantry management and timely reminders.

### The Solution
WasteLess Smart Kitchen provides a simple yet powerful digital pantry management tool that helps users avoid food waste and stay organized:
-Users can add food items with name, quantity, and expiry date so nothing gets forgotten.
-The app calculates and displays the status of items dynamically (Fresh / At Risk / Expired) based on real dates.
-It gives users predictive insights about how many items are fresh or near expiry and highlights those that need attention.
-Through its vibrant, animated UI and status indicators, users get intuitive reminders of what needs to be used soon ‚Äî a form of practical expiry alerting similar to smart pantry trackers that help households reduce food waste by monitoring expiration dates and issuing alerts.
This solution turns manual tracking (notes or memory) into a smart, visual, and real‚Äëtime digital experience that encourages mindful use of food and helps reduce everyday household waste.
